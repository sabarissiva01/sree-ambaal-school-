About us


Sree Ambaal's Matriculation Higher Secondary School is providing secondary level education and is being managed by private unaided organisation. Sree Ambaal's Matriculation Higher Secondary School,started in 2001,is affiliated with state board for secondary level education system. The student teacher ratio of Sree Ambaal's Matriculation Higher Secondary School can be approximated to be 1 teacher for every 24 students.Sree Ambaal's Matriculation Higher Secondary School which is a name synonymous with student empowerment through excellence in their education.

Apart from various core subject teachers,we have an enthusiastic team of teachers for physical education, Yoga, Music, Dance, Karate and skating.
